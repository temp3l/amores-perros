# Bild-Prompts

Diese Prompts sind als direkte Arbeitsgrundlage für neue Hero-Bilder gedacht.

## Cartoon

| ID | Prompt |
| --- | --- |
| C1 | `Create a clean, warm cartoon hero illustration in 16:9 for a German dog training website. Show a smart dog in Hamburg on a quiet sidewalk, looking attentive and confident, with subtle city cues like bicycles and brick buildings. Use a restrained terracotta, cream, charcoal, and sand palette, lots of negative space for headline text, soft outlines, friendly editorial style, no text, no watermark.` |
| C2 | `Create a bright editorial cartoon illustration in 16:9 showing a smart dog and handler during a calm training conversation in a Hamburg neighborhood street. The dog should look clever and engaged, the handler relaxed and focused. Use modern flat shapes, gentle shading, terracotta accents, cream background, and clean space for page copy. No text, no logo, no watermark.` |
| C3 | `Create a sophisticated cartoon hero image in 16:9 with a smart dog sitting calmly beside a person in a small urban training setting in Hamburg. The scene should feel competent and trustworthy, not cute or childish. Use understated lines, warm muted colors, a little city context, and ample empty space for an overlaid headline. No text, no watermark.` |
