## Recommended Marketing Direction

### Structure recommendations

| Recommendation | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Separate “core training offers” from “community / events” and “professional offers” | The current homepage mixes very different buyer intents |
| Lead with Hamburg-specific messaging | The current site underuses its local advantage |
| Turn the Erstgespräch into the primary entry offer | It is already the clearest first-step product |
| Build trust with biography, qualifications, process, FAQs, and testimonials | The current tone is strong, but proof is thin |
