# Private Mantrailing Service
## Business Strategy, Marketing & Website Concept

Version: 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

The goal is to build a professional private mantrailing service that supports families and organizations during missing person incidents.

The service does **not** replace police, rescue organizations or official search and rescue teams. Instead, it provides rapid additional support where legally appropriate.

Core value proposition:

> Every minute matters.
> An experienced mantrailing dog can sometimes provide valuable information while time is critical.

The business should be positioned as a premium, trustworthy and professional service.

# Legal Positioning

The website should clearly state:

> We are a private mantrailing service.

> We do not replace police, rescue organizations or official search and rescue teams.

> In emergency situations always contact emergency services immediately.

Never advertise:

"We find missing people."

Instead:

"We assist with professional mantrailing."

or

"We support missing person searches."
